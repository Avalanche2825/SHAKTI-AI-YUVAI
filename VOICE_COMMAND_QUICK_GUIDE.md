## Troubleshooting

### Voice Commands Not Working?

**Check These:**

1. Is the green dot visible? (protection must be enabled)
2. Did you grant microphone permission?
3. Are you saying "HELP" loudly enough?
4. Is battery optimization disabled for the app?

**To Fix:**

- Go to Settings (type `777=`)
- Check all permissions are granted
- Disable battery optimization
- Restart the app and try again

### False Triggers (Accidental Activation)

**This is Rare Because:**

- Requires 3 distinct "HELP" commands
- Must be within 8 seconds
- Requires loud vocal energy
- Background noise filtered out

**If It Happens:**

- Cancel within 5 seconds
- Or let it record (better safe than sorry)
